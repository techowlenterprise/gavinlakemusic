Acoustic Singer, Songwriter & Artist in Nottingham

My first memories of music were sitting by the fire at my Aunt and Grandmother’s house listening to the radio. They would play northern soul and things like The Beatles and The Beach Boys and Percy Sledge just good soul and stuff from the sixties which I adored then and adore now.

As I grew older my tastes and passions grew from these roots and I started to listen to things like Bob Dylan and Neil Young and things like that.

As I developed my tastes I knew the only thing I wanted to do was be heavily involved in the creative arts. Painting, Poetry, Art; the whole gamut and I dedicated my life and my time to all these pursuits and these were the building blocks for what I do and am doing today.

Dreams? To me they are everything. I feel like world tries to crush dreams but like Bukowski said no matter how hard and painful life is you must keep that dying ember, that dream alive and keep it in your mind at all times because without it you are nothing.

I intend to let my ember burn on the become a wildfire because I simply live for love and the arts are our love. These are our dreams.

One must keep with us at all times. And one must never deter from them. This is the blueprint of our lives.
